Welcome to Delstarford Works ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. 2. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or device.

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address (for our Kakamega office coordination).
  • Account Data: Passwords (encrypted via Firebase), login timestamps, and usage logs.

We use the data we collect to engineer better solutions for you:

4. Data Storage and Security

We utilize industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Our backend is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Render) and our database utilizes Google Firebase's real-time encryption standards.

5. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following situations:
