DexaGen AI: Neuro-Symbolic Pharmacological Engine

How we reduced drug interaction testing time by 40% using Generative AI and DeepChem.

Client: MMUST Research
Duration: 6 Months
Tech: Python, TensorFlow, WebGL

The Challenge

Traditional drug discovery is slow and expensive. Researchers at MMUST needed a way to predict Drug-Drug Interactions (DDIs) without running thousands of physical wet-lab tests.

The Solution

We built DexaGen AI, a web-based platform that uses:

  • Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to analyze molecular structures.
  • 3D Visualization (using 3Dmol.js) to show binding pockets in real-time.
  • Generative AI to suggest safer molecular variations.
